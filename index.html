Welcome to the Online Yoga Space

My name is Alie, I am a 200 RYT qualified yoga teacher with a passion for sport and exercise, living in European State - Boston, USA.

My desire is to help people become mentally and physically balanced and build inner strength to deal with anything that life throws their way. For your convenience I create Online Yoga space that you can reach out your practice at any time and wherever you are in the world. Also you can join my in person classes in Boston.

I can’t wait to see you on the mat!
